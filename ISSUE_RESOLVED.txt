WHAT WAS WRONG:

  In CLIPS, you cannot retract a fact using a pattern match like:
    ❌ (retract (system-state (phase validation)))
  
  This causes: [EXPRNPSR3] Missing function declaration for 'system-state'
  
  You MUST use a fact handle obtained from the LHS:
    ✅ ?state <- (system-state (phase validation))
       ...
       (retract ?state)
